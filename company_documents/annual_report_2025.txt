==============================================================
         ACME CORPORATION — ANNUAL REPORT FY 2025
==============================================================

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
---------------------
Fiscal Year 2025 was a year of accelerated growth for ACME Corporation.
Total revenue reached $4.8 billion, an increase of 14% year-over-year.
Net income grew to $620 million (up 18%), driven by strong performance
in our Cloud Services and AI Products divisions.

Key highlights:
  • Revenue: $4.8 B (+14% YoY)
  • Net Income: $620 M (+18% YoY)
  • Operating Margin: 22.4% (up from 20.1%)
  • Employee Count: 28,500 (up from 24,000)
  • Customer Base: 12,000+ enterprise clients across 45 countries

2. REVENUE BREAKDOWN BY DIVISION
----------------------------------
Division                  Revenue      YoY Growth
Cloud Services            $1.92 B       +22%
AI & Data Products        $1.15 B       +31%
Enterprise Software       $0.96 B        +4%
Professional Services     $0.48 B        +6%
Hardware & IoT            $0.29 B        -3%

Cloud Services and AI Products together account for 64% of total
revenue, up from 55% in FY 2024. Hardware & IoT continues to decline
as the company shifts to software-centric offerings.

3. GEOGRAPHIC PERFORMANCE
--------------------------
Region              Revenue     YoY Growth
North America       $2.64 B      +12%
Europe              $1.10 B      +15%
Asia-Pacific        $0.82 B      +19%
Rest of World       $0.24 B      +10%

Asia-Pacific showed the strongest growth, propelled by new data-centre
partnerships in India, Japan, and Australia.

4. KEY RISKS AND CHALLENGES
-----------------------------
  • Regulatory Pressure: New AI-governance regulations in the EU and
    potential US federal legislation may increase compliance costs.
  • Talent Retention: Attrition in the engineering department rose to
    17% (from 13% in FY 2024), impacting delivery timelines.
  • Supply Chain: Semiconductor availability remains a risk for the
    Hardware & IoT division.
  • Currency Fluctuations: A stronger US dollar reduced European revenue
    by approximately $45 M when expressed in USD.

5. OUTLOOK FOR FY 2026
------------------------
Management projects revenue of $5.4–5.6 billion for FY 2026
(12–17% growth). Priority investment areas include:
  • Expanding the AI Products portfolio with generative-AI solutions.
  • Opening two new data centres in Southeast Asia.
  • Launching a dedicated Cybersecurity division in Q2 2026.
  • Reducing engineering attrition to below 12% through compensation
    and career-path improvements.
