==============================================================
       ACME CORPORATION — Q3 FY 2025 QUARTERLY REPORT
==============================================================

PERIOD: July 1 – September 30, 2025

1. FINANCIAL SUMMARY
----------------------
  • Quarterly Revenue: $1.28 B (+16% YoY, +4% QoQ)
  • Gross Margin: 61.3%
  • Operating Income: $285 M
  • Free Cash Flow: $210 M

2. DIVISIONAL HIGHLIGHTS
--------------------------
Cloud Services ($510 M, +24% YoY)
  – Signed 340 new enterprise contracts.
  – Launched ACME CloudGuard, a zero-trust security add-on.

AI & Data Products ($320 M, +35% YoY)
  – Released ACME GenStudio v2.0 with multi-modal capabilities.
  – Secured a $45 M deal with a Fortune-50 healthcare firm.

Enterprise Software ($250 M, +3% YoY)
  – ERP platform migration to cloud completed for 60% of clients.
  – Customer satisfaction (CSAT) score improved to 82 (from 78).

Professional Services ($128 M, +5% YoY)
  – Utilisation rate: 74%.
  – Launched an AI-advisory practice generating $12 M in Q3.

Hardware & IoT ($72 M, -5% YoY)
  – Continued decline; management evaluating strategic options
    including potential divestiture in FY 2026.

3. KEY RISKS IDENTIFIED THIS QUARTER
---------------------------------------
  • Engineering Attrition: Hit 18% annualised in Q3 (up from 15% in Q2).
    Root-cause analysis points to below-market compensation in
    mid-level roles and limited remote-work flexibility.
  • Data-Centre Capacity: US-East region operating at 91% utilisation;
    expansion not online until Q1 FY 2026. Risk of SLA breaches if
    demand spikes unexpectedly.
  • Regulatory: EU AI Act enforcement begins Q4 — compliance readiness
    at 70%. Estimated remaining investment: $8 M.
  • Client Concentration: Top-10 clients represent 28% of revenue.

4. EMPLOYEE & CULTURE METRICS
-------------------------------
  • Headcount: 27,800 (Q2: 27,200)
  • Offer-acceptance rate: 68% (target 80%)
  • Diversity: Women in tech roles — 31% (target 35%)
  • eNPS (Employee Net Promoter Score): 42 (industry avg: 38)

5. OUTLOOK FOR Q4 FY 2025
---------------------------
  – Expected revenue: $1.30–1.35 B
  – Planned product launches: ACME DataMesh (real-time analytics)
  – Board to finalise Cybersecurity division charter in November.
  – Engineering retention task-force to present action plan by Oct 31.
