==============================================================
       ACME CORPORATION — STRATEGIC PLANNING NOTES
              (Leadership Off-site, August 2025)
==============================================================

ATTENDEES: CEO, CFO, CTO, COO, CHRO, Division Heads

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THEME 1 — DOUBLE DOWN ON AI
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • GenAI is the largest growth lever. Invest additional $150 M
    in FY 2026 R&D budget for AI Products division.
  • Acquire or partner with two AI-native startups (target:
    NLP/LLM tooling and computer-vision verticals).
  • Internal AI adoption: deploy co-pilot tools for Engineering,
    Sales, and Customer Support by Q2 2026.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THEME 2 — GEOGRAPHIC EXPANSION
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Open Singapore data centre (H1 2026) and Mumbai data centre
    (H2 2026) to serve APAC growth.
  • Establish regional HQs in Singapore and Dubai.
  • Localise products for Japanese and Korean markets.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THEME 3 — PORTFOLIO RATIONALISATION
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Hardware & IoT: Explore divestiture or spin-off; division is
    margin-dilutive (-1.2% operating margin) and non-strategic.
  • Enterprise Software: Accelerate cloud migration; sunset
    on-premise licensing by end of FY 2027.
  • Professional Services: Grow AI-advisory sub-practice to $80 M
    run-rate by end of FY 2026.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THEME 4 — TALENT & CULTURE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Address engineering attrition (currently 17-18%):
      – Benchmark compensation to 75th percentile of market.
      – Expand remote-work policy to 3 days/week.
      – Launch "Tech Fellows" programme for senior ICs.
  • Increase women in tech roles from 31% → 36% by FY 2027.
  • Establish AI-literacy training for all employees (target:
    80% completion by Q4 2026).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THEME 5 — CYBERSECURITY AS A NEW PILLAR
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Stand up a dedicated Cybersecurity division in Q2 2026.
  • Seed investment: $50 M + potential tuck-in acquisition.
  • Target: $200 M revenue run-rate within 3 years.
  • Leverage existing CloudGuard product as anchor offering.
